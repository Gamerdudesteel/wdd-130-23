Overview

Purpose

My purpose for this website is to provide ratings and reviews of games, movies and tv shows from a conservative point of view. My idea for this website came about when wired published their obviously bias review of hogwarts legacy and rated it a 1/10 despite the game recieving near universal acclaim. this low score was obviosly motivated by Jk rowlings view that men and women are different and not about the game itself. This kind of "journalism" is not only tolerated but featured on the google review scores. My goal is to combat these types of dishonest and politically motivated reviews which are not only in gaming but in moves, for instance if you look at rotten tomatoes you will see the obvious devide in opinion between "critics" and moviegoers, for instance "star wars: the last jedi" was rated 91% by "critics" and 42% by people who werent bribed into writing good reviews by disney.

Audience

My audience is anyone who is tired of mainstream media rags and wants news, rumors, leaks, and reviews from a different, objectively better perspective.
