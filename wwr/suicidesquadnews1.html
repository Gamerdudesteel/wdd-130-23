The suicide squad game is going to SUCK

EIGHT years for THIS?

Rocksteady made masterpieces. When rocksteady was approached by warner bros to make a batman game, they only had ONE game under their belt; Urban chaos: riot response, a 2006 first person shooter. Warner bros liked the game SO much that they trusted rocksteady with their most valuable i.p batman. Unlike urban chaos, the batman series were third person games where you don't use guns. These games were perfect. Yes perfect. They were nothing short of masterpieces. I can't tell you how good they are so go play them yourself (I finished every game in the series twice and city 3 times). The arkhman games were released from 2009 to 2015, thats 7 years. in 2020, 5 years later, suicide squad: Kill the justice leauge was announced. the next year it was announced for a 2022 release, the next year it was delayed to 2023, a total of 8 years since arkham knight. now there's rumors of a SECOND delay. Why could that be? because the game sucks and they know it. When the first full 15 minuets gameplay trailer was released it was FLOODED with dislikes and negative comments. most of these comments repeat the same complaints: All the charecters play the same, why does a shark use a gun? why does harley quin have such an annoying voice? was arleen sorkin not good enough? how does anyone stand doing prolonged recording sessions with tara strong? The gameplay consists of shooting purple blobs, why do all the charecters fly? how dare you put microtransations in a 70 dollar game? why does captain boomerang use a gun instead of a boomerang? why does it need to be online for singleplayer? The most important question is how could you take EIGHT YEARS to make THIS? it's infuriating to fans. as of the time i'm writing this the trailer on the official playstation channel has 9 thousand likes and 51 thousand dislikes. This is why youtube disabled visible dislikes, not for "small creators". The intense hatred for cheap soulless live service cash grab GARBAGE is clear! so what can a game company do? delay it! again! not to fix the baked in issues that plauge the game (shoot purple blobs, numbers go up, charecters are the same)no, it's too late for that, all they can do now is move the game to a less crowded release window so they don't have to compete with the legend of zelda. Honestly the choice is either make a little bit of money or make no money and if they stick with the current release date it will be the latter. It's obvious that rocksteady didn't want to make this game, after all BOTH of the companies founders Sefton Hill and Jamie Walker left the company near the end of 2022. They had previously worked for argnaut games which closed in 2004 (fun fact; argounats last game was based on the catwoman movie, meaning arkham city wasn't jamie and Seftons first experience with the charecter) Jamie and Sefton did't leave the company for nothing. But they wouldn't exatly say why either. It's my personal belief that with their contractual obligations complete on suicide squad, they chose to take what was left of their dignity elsewhere. I guarentee that this live service bs was thought up not by rocksteady but by out of touch warner brothers exectutives and their greedy shareholders. This game will suck and fail and rocksteady will fall as a result. How hard is yet to be seen.
